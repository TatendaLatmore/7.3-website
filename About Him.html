Osamason, or Amari Middelton was born may 20th 2003. Rap's rising star attracts audiences with his razor-sharp lyricism and captivating stage presence. His rhymes, despite coming from the streets, are a monument to the human spirit's tenacity and greatness. Osamason has a flow that is both strong and adaptable, moving through beats with a grace that demands attention. He stays grounded, however, and channels his experiences into lyrics that ring true and authentic despite the glitz and glamour of the music business. With a style of storytelling that verges on the cinematic, Osamason draws his audience into his universe, where suffering and success coexist to produce a symphony of feeling. One thing is certain as he continues to forge his way in the rap game: Osamason is not just a rapper but a force to be reckoned with, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts and minds of all who encounter his artistry.
